Tech-fest 2K21

Rustamji Institute of Technology, BSF Tekanpur is going to organise an inter college Techfest FESTOBER -2K21 on 25-27 October 2021 in virtual mode. Attractive prizes to win in every event. E-Certificates for all participants. To participate, please register here

There is no participation fees.

Events and Competitions

Hackathon

Choose a topic, form a team, prepare a PPT, get shortlisted, work on the project and…. Yup that’s it Win the hackathon if your project stands up among the rest. It is a Web/Mobile-App/AI-ML based project development competition… where students can participate in a team of maximum 4 members, present their idea through a ppt, get shortlisted, develop the project and compete in the hackathon.

Coding-Competition

It will be a DSA and CP based coding competition. It will consist of 5 questions which must be solved within 3hrs. Time will be a key factor in deciding ranks. This contest will be hosted on Codechef. You must be registered on Codechef to participate. Winner will get attractive prize.

Video Shooting (Rjit Only)

This event is for Rjit students only. Students have to make the video related to campus and submit and those who make good video will be winner and awarded attractive prices.

Games

It's BGMI your favourite one's. A custom room erangle map squad competition. Get yourself registered and participate in the competition winning squad will get exciting prize.

Photo-Editing

This is the best competition to showcase your photo editing skills. You will be given an initial image you will get some time to edit it and then submit your edited image and win the competition if jury loved it.

Poster-making

IThis is the best competition to showcase your poster making skills. You will be given a theme to make poster and you have to make it and submit it and winners will get attractive prizes.

Idea Pitching

At the time of event you will be given a theme and how far you pitch idea related to theme.

Tech-Vita

A technical quiz organized by our sponsors.
